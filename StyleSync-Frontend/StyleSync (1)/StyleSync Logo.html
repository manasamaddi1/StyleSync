StyleSync — six little marks

Each one tries a different angle on "your closet, softer." Pick one (or mash up parts) and we'll roll it into the app.
01 Hanger Loop
Hand-drawn hanger, terracotta knot at the hook. Italic Fraunces wordmark, small Space Grotesk tagline. Reads as friendly, not fashion-y.
02 Threaded Monogram
Sage circle mark holding an S, terracotta dashed thread looping out like it's been stitched. Works as a standalone app icon.
03 Folded Shirt
A neatly-folded tee with a terracotta seam and sage band. Quiet, domestic, a little Muji-like.
04 Sunday Sun
Butter-yellow sun behind a terracotta tee. Optimistic, warm, very "Sunday morning."
05 Quiet Wordmark
Pure type. style in italic Fraunces, sync. stacked underneath in upright bold terracotta — separated by a sage hairline. Nothing extra.
06 Soft Knot
Two soft loops in sage + terracotta — your closet and the day, quietly tied. Most abstract; reads beautifully tiny as a favicon.
